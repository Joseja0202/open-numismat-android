Welcome

With OpenNumismat for Android your coins collection always in your device. You can use existent catalogues or create your own catalogue with desktop version of OpenNumismat.

This is a mobile version of OpenNumismat that helps track of which coins you already have.

Available catalogues:

  • commemorative Euro coins
  • commemorative USA coins
  • commemorative Russian coins (русский язык)
